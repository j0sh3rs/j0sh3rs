## 🧬 Who I am

Platform/DevOps engineer and recovering EM who spends the workday optimizing other people's time and the evenings breaking my own homelab so I can fix it again. I care about tooling that sparks joy, mentoring people into loving the craft, and arguing about whether **Nightcrawler** is the most underrated X-Man (he is).

When I'm not wrangling clusters: re-reading **Marvel** runs, yelling at a **soccer** match, or falling down an **existential-philosophy** rabbit hole and pretending I understand Heidegger.

## 🎯 What I'm into

- 🦾 **Marvel Comics** — X-Men first, always. Spider-Man, Legion, and Nightcrawler get the rest of my attention.
- ⚽ **Soccer** — World Cup obsessive. Messi > Ronaldo, and I will die on this hill.
- 🧠 **Existential philosophy** — Wittgenstein, Heidegger, and Gadamer live in my head rent-free.
- 🏠 **Homelab tinkering** — a Kubernetes cluster I absolutely did not need, run purely for the love of the game.

## 🛠️ What I tinker with

**☸️ Kubernetes & GitOps** — k3s/k8s at home, Flux-driven GitOps, Helm, the whole declarative circus ([home-ops](https://github.com/j0sh3rs/home-ops), [home-cluster](https://github.com/j0sh3rs/home-cluster))

**🤖 Self-hosted AI** — local LLM stack with LiteLLM, llama-swap, n8n automation, and MCP servers running on my own metal

**🌐 Networking & TLS** — Traefik, Cilium, Cloudflare tunnels, and an unhealthy enthusiasm for certificates ([cfaccess-proxy](https://github.com/j0sh3rs/cfaccess-proxy))

**📊 Monitoring & observability** — Prometheus, Grafana, Netdata, and tuning alerts until the false positives stop screaming
